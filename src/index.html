Eladio Carrión @eladioMusic_7

Eladio Carrión is a Puerto Rican singer, songwriter and music producer known for his versatility in genres such as reggaeton, trap and hip-hop. Born on April 24, 1994 in Carolina, Puerto Rico, he has stood out in the urban music scene with his distinctive style and ability to combine catchy lyrics with innovative rhythms.
